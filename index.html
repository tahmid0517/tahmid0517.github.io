About Me

I'm a 1B Computer Science student at the University of Waterloo.

I've programmed quite a bit over the past few years and created a number of projects which you can find here and on my Github.

Throughout high school, I was immersed in the FIRST Robotics Competition, leading my team in the areas of programming, CAD and fundraising. In grades 11 and 12, I was the overall team captain and lead my team to 2 consecutive World Championship berths.

At my school's Commencement Ceremony, in recognition of my work as a part of the school's robotics team, I was awarded the Principal's Award for Student Leadership.
